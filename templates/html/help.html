Moves

Attack {input,attack} Regular attack deals 2 dmg

Kick {input,run} Use kick to fall enemy down or counter rolling. Kick 1 dmg or 2 dmg if the enemy is rolling.

Block {input,block} Block attacks, kicks, knives and waves. Block can be broken by rolling. On successful block you can perform a quick counter-attack.

Roll {input,roll} Use roll to break enemy block - it also allows you to sneak under the low holes in the wall.

Run Hold {input,run} Hold right mouse button to run using stamina.

Special {input,spell1} By performing this combination you can use your special skill once in a while.

Jump {input,jump} Use jump to avoid rolling attack and wave. You can also jump through the window and over the fissures. You can perform a long jump while running.

Throw weapon {input,throw_weapon} You can throw your main weapon - but you will be disarmed for 15 seconds.

Dash Double tap move Once per 5 seconds you can use a dash to dodge an attack.

Pro tips

Block + Dash Break block If you dash while blocking you can break enemy shield.

Use wave Disabling mines If you hit a ticking mine with a wave - you can disable and pick it up

Use kick Reversing knives You can reverse the knife if you kick it

Throw items Support You can give an item to a friend by holding the button that normally uses it.

Combat Advanced If you want to stand a chance against experienced players you have to understand blocking and counter-attack.

Moves Advanced You are moving slower if not facing movement direction. You will also move slower when blocking
